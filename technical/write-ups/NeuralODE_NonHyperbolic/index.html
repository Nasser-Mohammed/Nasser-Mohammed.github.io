Structural Instability of Neural ODEs near Saddle-Node Bifurcations

Introduction

Neural ODEs are useful for systems that involve in continuous time, but whose underlying dynamics are unknown or difficult to derive. Furthermore, the excess of data available today makes the data-driven approach of Neural ODEs viable. However, there are some systems where Neural ODEs struggle to accurately model. One such class of systems are those with non-hyperbolic equilibria.
